World Best All-rounder

Shakib Al Hasan

Bangladeshi Cricketer

Shakib Al Hasan (Bengali: সাকিব আল হাসান; born 24 March 1987) is a Bangladeshi international cricketer and philanthropist. Shakib is considered to be one of the greatest all-rounders of all times.[2][3][4][5][6][7][8] He was ranked as one of the world's most famous athletes by ESPN World Fame 100 in 2019.[9] His aggressive left-handed batting style in the middle order, controlled slow left-arm orthodox bowling has made him a consistent player for Bangladesh.
